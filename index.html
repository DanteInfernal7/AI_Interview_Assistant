Frequently Asked Questions

Read the FAQ's below to know more about ASSISTENTIA

  • How does ASSESTENTIA work?

    ASSISTENTIA is Spanish for assistance. The main goal of our website is to help students prepare for interviews and get their dream job. We provide services like resume analyzer, emotion detection and speech analyzer to help students understand their weak points and improve on them.

  • What is resume analyzer?

    Resume analyzer is a feature which detects any unprofessional language in your resume. You can upload your resume and our python scripts will suggest improvements that can be made to help your resume stand out among the rest of the applicants.

  • What is emotion detection?

    You can upload a small video of yourself answering basic interview questions like “Tell us something about yourself” and we will run your video through a python script which will detect your emotions while answering these questions. This data is then used to provide detailed analysis of your answer. For example, if you are displaying a specific emotion like fear or anger which is not ideal in an interview, ASSISTENTIA will analyze that and give you feedback accordingly.

  • How does the feedback feature work?

    Once you have uploaded a video of yourself answering the questions, our website will generate a report based on things that you were good at and the things that you might need to work on. We will use emotion detection and speech to text analyzer to give you proper feedback.

  • Do we have to pay to use ASSISTENTIA?

    ASISTENCIA is currently a free-to-use website. However, if you like our services, we do accept donations through patreon.
